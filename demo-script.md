# Demo Script

1. Open **MacroScout Helper Console** and read the **What this page does** block so viewers know the full flow.
2. In **Step 1: Enter your setup details**, fill in Service URL and Access key, then mention that App name and Helper name identify this run.
3. In **Step 2: Choose content to open**, paste a Content URL and pick **Summary** or **Full article**.
4. (Optional) Set **Spending limit in micro-units** to show cost control in the demo.
5. Click **Create usage credit** in Step 3 and wait for **Usage credit created successfully**.
6. Point out the **Usage credit code** block and click **Copy usage credit code** to show the value can be reused in demos.
7. Click **Open content** in Step 4 and confirm the **Content loaded successfully** message.
8. Show the **Content preview** panel so viewers can see immediate output.
9. Move to **Step 5: Review your receipt** and explain that the receipt is proof of what happened.
10. Click **Copy receipt details** and note that the copied text includes Helper name, source, amount, reference ID, and time.
11. In **Step 6: Verify in reporting tools**, open **Open buyer view** and **Open publisher view** and compare reference ID, amount, and time.
12. Return to **Recent activity (last 5)** and click a row to demonstrate quick setup reload for repeat demos.
