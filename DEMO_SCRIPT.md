# MacroScout Demo Script (60 to 90 seconds)

1. Open the page and say: "This is MacroScout Research Agent. It fetches premium research through FairFetch licensing and writes a receipt for every paid retrieval."
2. In **Demo run**, pick a target report and keep **Use licensed access marker** on.
3. Set license to **SUMMARY** and mention the max price guardrail.
4. Click **Run licensed fetch**.
5. Narrate the progress steps as they complete:
   - Quote and mint token
   - Fetch content
   - Receipt written
6. Point to **Returned content** and call out the label **Licensed AI view**.
7. Point to the **Receipt** card and read txId and priceMicros.
8. Click **Copy proof block** and mention it includes agent identity, URL, license, spend, and timestamp.
9. Move to **Show this next** and copy txId to compare with Publisher Transactions.
10. Open **Advanced controls** to show backend setup, masked key handling, request details, and technical diagnostics.
11. Close with **Recent runs** and click a prior run to show instant replay without another backend call.

Optional variation:
- Turn off **Use licensed access marker** and run again when supported by the publisher route.
- Call out the label change to **Human paywall preview**.
